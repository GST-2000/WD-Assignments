Air Pollution Prediction Using Deep Learning

The goal of this Project is to learn more about the Air Quality Index (AQI), which determines whether or not the air around us is contaminated. I considered employing a hybrid model based on CNN and LSTM to estimate PM2.5 levels of air pollution in Beijing's urban region. For correlation, we used the data of stations. The spatial properties and internal characteristics between different factors were extracted using CNN, and the prediction effect was improved using LSTM. The primary conclusions of this work are that by using CNN and LSTM, we can obtain the spatial and temporal aspects of the data with high accuracy and stability. Published IEEE paper in 2022 IEEE 2nd Mysore Sub Section International Conference (MysuruCon).
